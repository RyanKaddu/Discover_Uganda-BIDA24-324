Featured Destinations

Explore Uganda's most breathtaking locations that attract visitors from around the world

Bwindi Impenetrable Forest

Home to nearly half of the world's remaining mountain gorillas, this UNESCO World Heritage Site offers unforgettable gorilla trekking experiences.

Learn More →

Murchison Falls

Witness the Nile River forced through a narrow gorge before plunging 43 meters, creating one of Africa's most powerful waterfalls.
